Here identification with characters, which we as story-consumers naturally grasp for and storyteller helpfully provide, plays with suspense and our expectations. We know enough about Rick to know that he is not a bad man, but not enough to know.... whether he will another man. We also know enough about his possible victims to not like him all that much, but not enough to actually wish him dead. The film extends this excruciating waiting game as long as possible with long following shots and meandering conversations.

And appropriately this short, gritty thriller ends at an old and decaying outdoor theater in the middle of the forest.
